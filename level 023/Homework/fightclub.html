FIGHT CLUB

  • Title:Fight Club
  • Release Date: October 15, 1999
  • Director: David Fincher
  • Genres: Crime, Drama, Thriller
  • Runtime: 139 minutes

PLOT

An insomniac office worker (Edward Norton) forms an underground fight club with Tyler Durden (Brad Pitt), a charismatic soap maker. As the club's popularity grows, it evolves into something far more dangerous and anarchic.
