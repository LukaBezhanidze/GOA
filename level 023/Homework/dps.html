DEAD POET SOCIETY

  • Title:Dead Poets Society
  • Release Date: June 2, 1989
  • Director: Peter Weir
  • Genres: Comedy, Drama
  • Runtime: 128 minutes

PLOT

Set in 1959 at a conservative all-boys preparatory school, the film follows English teacher John Keating, who inspires his students to appreciate poetry, seize the day, and pursue their passions, challenging the school's strict traditions.
