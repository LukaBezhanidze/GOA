SOCIETY OF THE SNOW

  • Title:Society of the snow
  • Release date: January 4, 2024
  • Director: J.A. Bayona
  • Genres: Drama, History, Thriller
  • Runtime: 144 minutes

PLOT

On October 13, 1972, Uruguayan Air Force Flight 571, carrying a rugby team and their supporters, crashes into a glacier in the Andes Mountains. Of the 45 passengers, 29 survive the initial crash.
Facing extreme conditions, the survivors are forced to make harrowing decisions to stay alive.
